Professionals who embrace AI are seeing:

  • 20-40% productivity gains in knowledge work
  • Higher salaries - AI-skilled workers earn 15-25% more
  • Better job security - AI-augmented roles are harder to automate
  • New opportunities - Entirely new job categories emerging
